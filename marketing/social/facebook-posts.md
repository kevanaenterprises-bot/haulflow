## Posting Tips

- **Post timing:** Best engagement in trucking groups is 6-8 AM or 7-9 PM (when drivers are at home or on break)
- **Avoid:** Posting the same variant in multiple groups on the same day — space them out
- **Engagement:** Reply to every comment, especially skeptical ones. Authenticity wins.
- **Images:** Pair with a screenshot of the app or a simple phone-on-dash photo — NOT a stock image
- **CTA:** Always include the link but bury it naturally — don't make it the first line
