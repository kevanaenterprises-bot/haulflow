## LinkedIn Posting Strategy

- **Post timing:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7:30-8:30 AM or 12:00-1:00 PM (business audience)
- **Image:** Use a real screenshot or photo — avoid stock imagery. A fleet dashboard screenshot performs best on LinkedIn.
- **Engagement:** Reply to every comment within 2 hours. Tag commenters in follow-up replies.
- **Follow-up post (1 week later):** "Update: X founding slots remaining. Here's what the first users are saying..."
- **Hashtags:** Keep to 5-8 relevant tags. The ones above are well-trafficked in logistics LinkedIn.
- **Article companion:** Consider a LinkedIn Article version expanding on the ROI math (cost of current TMS vs. HaulFlow vs. manual spreadsheet hours).
- **Personal profile:** Ensure the poster's LinkedIn headline includes something trucking-related for credibility.
