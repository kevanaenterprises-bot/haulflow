## Production Notes

- **Equipment:** Modern Android phone (Samsung Galaxy S-series, Pixel) for clean app footage. Use a second phone to record.
- **Stabilization:** Use a simple phone mount or gimbal. Shaky footage kills credibility.
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred. Overhead fluorescent kills the look. Shoot near the truck for good context.
- **Audio:** Lapel mic if possible. Phone audio works but a cheap wireless mic ($20-30) is a massive upgrade.
- **App footage:** Use screen recording (Android built-in) and overlay it, or physically show the phone screen (more authentic).
- **Captioning:** Always add captions — 80%+ of short-form video is watched on mute.
- **Pacing:** Cut aggressively. Remove every "um," pause, and dead air. Under 30 seconds means tight.
- **Hook test:** If the first 3 seconds don't make someone stop scrolling, reshoot the hook. The hook is everything.
- **CTA placement:** Link in bio (TikTok/IG) or pinned comment (YouTube Shorts). Never put a raw URL in the video itself.
